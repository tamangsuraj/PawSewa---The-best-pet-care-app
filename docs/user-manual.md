# Appendix D: PawSewa User Manual

**Audience:** Pet owners using the PawSewa mobile app (Android / iOS)  
**Version:** 1.0 · May 2026

This guide covers the main tasks in the app. For help, use **Messages → Customer Care** or contact support through the app.

---

## 1. Getting started — Registration & login

### Create an account
1. Open the PawSewa app and tap **Sign up** / **Register**.
2. Enter your name, email, phone, and password.
3. Tap **Register**. You will see a confirmation that an OTP was sent to your email.
4. Enter the **6-digit OTP** on the verification screen to activate your account.
5. After verification, you are signed in and taken to your **Home** dashboard.

### Log in
1. On the login screen, enter your **email** and **password**.
2. Tap **Login**. You can also sign in with **Google** if enabled.
3. If you forgot your password, tap **Forgot Password?**, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.

### Tips
- Keep your email verified — some features require a verified account.
- Allow **notifications** when prompted so you receive appointment and reminder alerts.

---

## 2. Adding & managing pets

### Add a pet
1. From **Home** or **My Pets**, tap **Add Pet** (+).
2. Fill in **name**, **species**, **breed**, **date of birth**, and **gender**.
3. Optionally upload a **photo**.
4. Tap **Save**. Each pet receives a unique **PawID** for tracking across visits.

### View or edit a pet
1. Open **My Pets** and tap a pet card.
2. On **Pet Details**, review profile, health summary, and quick actions.
3. Tap the **edit (pencil)** icon to update name, breed, photo, or notes.
4. Use the **menu (⋮)** to **Delete pet** (permanent — confirm carefully).

---

## 3. Booking appointments

PawSewa supports **home visits** (vet comes to you) and related service requests through a guided flow.

### Book a new visit
1. Go to **Services** from the bottom navigation or Home.
2. Tap **Book New Appointment**.
3. Follow the guided steps:
   - **Select pet**
   - **Choose service type** (e.g. health checkup, vaccination, consultation)
   - **Pick date & time window**
   - **Confirm location** (GPS required — allow location permission)
   - **Review & submit**
4. After submission, status shows as **Pending** until a vet is assigned to your zone.

### Track your booking
1. Open **My Requests** / **Service Requests**.
2. View status: **Pending → Assigned → Accepted → En route → Completed**.
3. When a vet is **Accepted** or **Assigned**, tap **Message Vet** to chat about the visit.
4. Use **Live tracking** (when available) to see the vet’s approach on the map.

### Cancel or reschedule
- Open the request and use **Cancel** if shown, or book a new slot after cancelling.
- You cannot book two visits for the same pet within **2 hours** of an existing booking.

## 4. Subscribing to care plans

Care plans give bundled benefits such as home consultations and vaccination reminders.

### View plans
1. From **Home**, open **Subscription** / **Care Plans** (or the Pro/subscription entry point).
2. On the **Plans** tab, browse **Basic** and **Premium** options (monthly or annual).
3. Each card shows price, billing cycle, and included services.

### Subscribe
1. Tap **Subscribe** on your chosen plan.
2. Complete payment via **Khalti** (or the payment method shown).
3. On success, your plan appears under **My Subscription**.

### Manage subscription
1. Switch to the **My Subscription** tab.
2. View plan name, price, start/end dates, and status (**Active**, **Cancelling**, **Expired**).
3. Tap **Cancel Plan** to cancel — access continues until the current billing period ends.

## 5. Using the shop

Buy pet food, supplies, and accessories from verified sellers.

### Browse & buy
1. Open **Shop** from the bottom navigation.
2. Browse categories or search products.
3. Tap a product to view details. **Out of stock** items are greyed out and cannot be added.
4. Tap **Add to basket**, choose quantity, and confirm.
5. Open the **cart**, review items, apply a **promo code** if you have one, and proceed to **Checkout**.

### Pay & track orders
1. Select or add a **delivery address**.
2. Pay with **Khalti** (or eSewa where available).
3. View order status under **My Orders**: confirmed → preparing → out for delivery → delivered.
4. Tap **Chat with Seller** on a product if you have questions before buying.

---

## 6. Accessing health records

Your pet’s clinical history builds automatically as vets complete visits.

### View medical history
1. Open a **Pet Details** screen or tap **Health Records** / **Medical History** for that pet.
2. The timeline lists past visits, diagnoses, prescriptions, and notes (newest first).
3. Tap an entry to open the **report detail** view.
4. Pull down to **refresh** — new vet entries appear automatically when submitted.

### Vaccination reminders
- **Home** may show health alerts and due dates for vaccines or checkups.
- Tap **Book vaccine** on an alert to schedule the visit directly.

---

## 7. Initiating consultations

### Message your assigned vet (appointment chat)
1. After a vet is **Assigned** or **Accepted** on a service request, open **My Requests**.
2. Tap **Message Vet** on that booking.
3. Send text messages; use call options in the chat bar for audio/video when available.

### Direct vet chat (from Messages)
1. Open **Messages** from the bottom navigation.
2. Select a vet you have previously interacted with, or start from a completed visit link.
3. Chat about follow-ups, prescriptions, or general questions tied to your pet.

### Customer Care
1. In **Messages**, open **Customer Care** for platform support (not clinical emergencies).
2. For urgent pet emergencies, contact a local clinic or emergency vet directly — PawSewa is not an emergency hotline.
